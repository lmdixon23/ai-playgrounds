AI Playgrounds

Twelve interactive visualizations of the core ideas in any introductory AI course. Drag the sliders. Click the canvas. Watch the algorithms run, step by step.

Search

Pathfinding Visualizer

BFS, DFS, and A* race across a grid you draw. Watch the search frontier expand in real time.

→ Why A* explores fewer nodes than BFS
Logical agent

Wumpus World

The classic Russell & Norvig environment. Watch a logical agent infer safe squares from percepts — world view next to the agent's knowledge map.

→ The difference between "I haven't seen it" and "I've proved it's not there"
Logic & knowledge bases

CNF & SAT Builder

Type propositional sentences. Watch them get rewritten into CNF, step by step. Brute-force SAT-checks the formula and shows every satisfying model.

→ Why every SAT solver in the world wants CNF as input
Probability

Bayes Rule Playground

Slide the prior. Slide the accuracy. See how a "99% accurate" test changes its mind about a rare disease.

→ Why intuition about probability is usually wrong
Bayesian networks

Bayesian Network

The R&N burglar-earthquake network. Editable CPTs, exact inference, Gibbs sampling. See the "explaining away" effect live.

→ Why learning of an earthquake LOWERS your belief in a burglary
Supervised learning

K-Nearest Neighbors

Click to add training points of either class. Watch the decision boundary redraw itself as you change k.

→ What "k" actually does to a model
Evaluation

Overfitting Explorer

Fit polynomials from degree 1 to degree 20. Watch train error drop while test error explodes.

→ Why a perfect training fit can fail in the real world
Local search

Hill Climbing & SA

Six algorithm variants × three problems (TSP, N-queens, function landscape). Watch greedy methods get stuck while simulated annealing escapes.

→ Why sometimes you have to take a worse step
Neural networks

Tiny Neural Net

Train a small network to classify points. Add neurons, engineer input features (x², x·y, sin), change the activation, and watch the weights move as a live network graph while the loss falls.

→ How layers + non-linearity carve up space
Unsupervised learning

K-Means Clustering

Drop points, set k, then step through assignments and updates. The whole algorithm in one screen.

→ What "iterating to convergence" looks like
Vision

Convolution Playground

Draw on a canvas, pick a 3×3 filter, and see it detect edges, blur, or sharpen — then watch a random filter learn itself by gradient descent.

→ Why a tiny matrix can "see" features
Reinforcement learning

Q-Learning Gridworld

An agent learns to find the goal by trial and error. Watch its Q-values fill in across the grid as it plays.

→ How a value function emerges from random walking
