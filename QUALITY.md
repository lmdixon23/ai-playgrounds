# How the project works

AI Playgrounds is designed around portable classroom use, inspectable software, and clear evidence limits.

## Built into the project

- Twelve bilingual, offline-ready applets
- One focused concept per applet
- Featured experiments and scenario comparisons
- Visual mechanism explanations
- Teacher misconception and fidelity notes
- Local student response packets
- Keyboard guidance and text-state descriptions
- Algorithmic regression tests
- Automated browser checks across three viewport sizes

## Privacy

The site has no student accounts, backend, database, or student-upload endpoint. Response text stays in the learner's browser until the learner copies or prints it.

## Evidence limits

The software checks establish properties of the artifact. They do not establish learning gains, classroom adoption, accessibility conformance, or educational impact. Those questions require separate human and learner evidence.
